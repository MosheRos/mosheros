Our Mission

Our mission is to drive awareness of the importance of giving Maaser (tithe) to those who need it most. Since 2019, Imaaser has facilitated millions of dollars in donations to charities and non-profits by providing tools and halachic advice to make Maaser management simple and meaningful.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Maaser?

Maaser, or tithe, is the Jewish custom of donating 10% of one's earnings to charity. It is a way to give back to the community and support those in need.

How does the Imaaser app help?

The Imaaser app simplifies the process of tracking your income, calculating your maaser obligation, and distributing your donations. It provides a clear and organized way to manage your charitable giving.

Is this service free?

Yes, the Imaaser app and web dashboard are free to use. Our goal is to make it as easy as possible for everyone to participate in the mitzvah of Maaser.
